About Me

My mission is to bridge the gap between user needs and business goals—creating products that people genuinely like to use and fuel sustainable growth.

For over 15 years I've built teams that balance creative excellence with tangible results—transforming complex problems into clear, impactful experiences. I thrive in environments where I can elevate talent, maintain high standards, and create momentum for products that matter.

Leadership Principles

How I Work

Stay Close to the Work

Strong design leadership doesn't mean stepping away from craft. I stay hands-on in high-stakes moments—modeling standards and maintaining credibility with the team.

Clarity Over Completeness

When pressure is high, I focus on what matters most. Vision before optimization. Alignment before execution. Clear thinking unlocks better outcomes.

Build Trust Through Transparency

I explain tradeoffs clearly—what matters now, what can wait, and why. Honest communication builds the trust that enables teams to move fast.

Grow Others Deliberately

Mentorship isn't passive. I invest in developing designers through intentional coaching, challenging projects, and creating space for them to stretch.

Design for Business Impact

Great design serves users and the business. I focus on outcomes that matter—engagement, conversion, satisfaction—not just aesthetics.

Move with Intention

Speed without direction is just motion. I help teams build momentum by establishing clear vision and removing obstacles.
